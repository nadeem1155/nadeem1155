## About

Doctor of Philosophy in Information Technology from Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Lucknow (2019–2024). Research concentrated at the intersection of quantum computing, software security evaluation, and multi-criteria decision-making. Currently serving as Assistant Professor at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University, Lucknow, and as Associate Editor at PLOS ONE.
